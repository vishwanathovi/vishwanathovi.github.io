Forms and Tables are one of the few topics in HTML and CSS that are easy to get started with but to make them look nice, it requires good amount of effort.

According to the website's theme, we need to make sure that table and forms are consistent in design and also are responsive to work in different devices.

First we will look into form designing. Generally forms contain text, textareas, submit buttons. In some cases they might have drop downs, radio buttons and checkboxes.
