It's been almost a decade that i started using internet. I started using google, facebook and youtube. 

Back when google was just a search engine which gave you a good enough freedom to decide what you want to search and search the web for you. Facebook was that one place where you could see what your friends are upto. YouTube was one single place where you got access to the video content created across the world. 

Yes i sound stupid trying to explain three of the most used platforms across the globe but just bear with me. I just want to make a point that it is not the same now a days. In the name of super personalization and every companies is racing for the improvement in metrics like activity parameters and engagement parameters. 

In the last one year, i have been using Google, Gmail, Facebook, Linkedin, Amazon and Whatsapp on a regular basis. I would say Gmail and Whatsapp are still the tools that does not bother me and help get my work done. But the rest of the tools, they have become a absolute mirror of myself. 

Google remembers every single search of mine and tries to suggest everytime i open it.

Facebook, Linkedin, Amazon and YouTube are full of feed according to likes and activity.

All of these platforms have become this place for my comfort zone where they show the things i like over and over again. Due to this, using internet(Above specified platforms) more of a repetitive and less exploratory thing for me. 

Google ads that are so intelligent that every site i visit, they make sure to show things according to my activity in google, amazon and other ecommerce sites. 

Personalization is nessacery but it is becoming too much lately. 


Personalization: Information overload and inevitable ask for personalization. Subconsious decisions over consious ones.


Let's say you like football. When you start using YouTube you will start watching some football videos. What YouTube does is it suggests more football channels and videos. You want more of it because of this. This cycle goes on and all your YouTube feed becomes crowded by few of your strong interests.
