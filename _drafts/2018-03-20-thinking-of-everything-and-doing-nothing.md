20 to 30 is the age where we change a lot. We figure out our profession, meet our partners and kind of explore who we are and accept what we are. We learn to live in this society. We understand and adapt to the practices of society around us. the third decade of our life is also the most important decade(for most of us) on the skillset and knowledge we acquire. But for most of us, we end up wasting a lot of time in this period due to some common reasons. Today i wanted to discuss about few of them. Hope it helps some of the early 20 someones.

1. Thinking of everything and doing nothing
Remember the last time you made a list of things you will do from today. Get up early, workout, read books, learn new things, save money, get a better job. Most of the time we commit to too many things at once and then end up doing nothing. You can just think of last 2-3 years of yours and list down the things you did. And then list down the things you want to do in the next 6 months. You can see that you are overcommiting every single time and underperforming. Most people fall into this trap and waste their time with no success. 

I believe the reason for this being "fear of missing out". With a fast moving world, there are too many things in our life right now. So no one is ready to do just 2-3 things in a year. They want to do all. 

Best thing to solve this problem will be to accept the current you and accept to the gradual growth trajectory. Believe me, slow and steady wins the race. the best part about gradual growth is it's healthy and create miracles over the years of efforts. 


2. Doing things to please your parents, friends
" Do i really like doing it?"

This is the question you should ask to yourself about everything you do. Because some of the things we start doing in our early 20s is because of peer pressure, parental pressure. You shouldn't let these take away years out of your 20s. 

It is better to introspect and make some changes rather than just stay in that comfort zone.


3. Money is the most important thing
At the early 20s of your life, when you want to buy all the cool things around you and do cool things, it might look like money is the only thing that is required to be happy. But be aware that earning money(a lot of it) is a very hard job. If you start running behind the money, you will soon end up missing out on the important things in life.

Don't get me wrong here. Being financially safe and being aware of how ecomonomy works is important. But during your 20s money should just be an enabler not the goal itself.1
