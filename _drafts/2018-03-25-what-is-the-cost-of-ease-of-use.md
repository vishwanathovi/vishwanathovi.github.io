Concept: Explaining the sophestication that technology brings to our life and the corporate interests that follow them.


When we look back at the last 2 decades, technology has disrupted many aspects of our life. It has also affected the way we think and live. 

Mobile phones have changed the magnitude of access to technology.

Facebook, instagram, twitter, whatsapp have changed the way we use to interact. Now internet has become a place where you engage with friends and family the most than offline. Now we are totally comfortable share our life moments, likes-dislikes online. We have become more open to meet new people online based on similar interests and viewpoints.

E-commerce companies like Amazon, Flipkart have changed the way we buy things. Uber, Ola have changed the way we travel. PayTm, Netbanking have changed the way we handle finance. Netflix, Hotstar are changing the way we consume content. 


I wouldn't want to comment the above changes in our society as a positive or a negative thing. I would just say that it is the way the society is advancing. 

But one thing that we have to notice is the huge corporate interest behind these technology companies. We can see that billions of dollars are being poured into these technology companies. These investments are done knowing that these companies will generate billions in the coming years and decades.



If some 50 years before, someone told me that television media is going to be huge and content will be valued a lot, i would just deny the magnitute of it. Now we can see how it panned out. We can see how powerful visual media grew into and how much of power it holds in the society.

When i see Google, Facebook, I see the same thing now but with a multifold of magnitude.
